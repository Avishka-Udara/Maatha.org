About Maatha World

Maatha World is a non-profit establishment created to bring together Sri Lankans living here and overseas in an effort to have have a "Safty Net" on the population of 20 million scattered into 14022 Grama Nildari Divisions around the Island nation,to make Sri Lanka economically self-reliant. Keeping Accountability, Transparency and Credibility at our core principles, the organization consists of Directors and Advisors who have provided a commendable service to the country with an admirable track record.

Mission

To have a Safety Net at a crisis situation and join hands from village level to build a Healthy and Harmonious Society.

Vision

Make Sri Lanka economically Self-reliant & Contented
